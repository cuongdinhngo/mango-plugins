# PROJ-401 — No horizontal scroll on any screen

**Requirement:** No screen may scroll horizontally; every screen reflows to fit narrow viewports.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- At the 320 px floor, no screen has horizontal scroll (`scrollWidth ≤ clientWidth` on the document
  root).

---

## Reachable surfaces (from the generated sitemap)

- the overview route
- the reports route
- the settings route
- the filter overlay (full-window)
- the record-detail modal
